History Background

Orion Computers was founded in 2012 by a group of friends who specialisd in Information Technology. It aims to provide customers in South East Asia with reasonably priced gaming desktops and laptops.

Orion has had many failures however through commitment and perseverance, we managed to pull through and come up at the top. Now, Orion Computers has had 550,000 orders just from South East Asia from 2016 to 2019.

Within 2012 to the end of 2015, Orion faced many difficulties in tapping on the South East Asian market as many had no interest or were not well off to afford Gaming Desktops and Laptops even though a great majority of South East Asian residers were very into Gaming. This caused us to struggle with business expenses and the hopes of expansion.

However with the help of MNCs such as MSI, Gigabyte, etc and our hardwork in sourcing parts for the SEA market, we managed to finally tap into the market. Desktops and Laptops had a price reduction of nearly 70% as the parts that we import in with the help of numerous proposals to MNCs, were signifcantly cheaper.

Today, Orion is proud to be one of the top leading custom gaming desktops and laptops companies in South East Asia. Service, Excellence and Friendship is key!
