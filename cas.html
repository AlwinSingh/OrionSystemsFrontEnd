Service
To provide our customers with a better understanding of this service, we have provided a step by step guide on the process

Step 1: You will have to walk in at our Office (Address is located in the Contacts page). Having the components is optional. If you are looking to build a new PC but do not have the components, an agent will be assigned to you to assist you further.

Step 2: Our representative will allocate you the next available timeslot for the Custom Assembly Service. If you decide to go forth with the service, the process for the assembly will take about 1 business day or less, from the allocated timeslot given to you.

Step 3: An SMS will be sent to you to verify that your system is ready for collection. Drop by our office to make payment and pick it up!
